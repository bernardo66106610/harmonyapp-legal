Privacy Policy

Harmony with Yourself  ·  Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Harmony with Yourself mobile application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service. We follow a local-first approach — most of your data never leaves your device.

1. Who we are

Harmony with Yourself is an independent app developed by Sergey Ivanov. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at sergeynoma@gmail.com.

2. Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Mood check-in history
  • Journal entries (morning and evening reflections)
  • Practice completion records
  • Weekly question responses
  • App preferences and settings
  • Onboarding answers

This data is stored using Hive, an on-device database. It is not backed up to any cloud service by the App and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Data sent to third-party services

Firebase (Google LLC)
We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the App is used and to detect crashes. Firebase may collect:

  • Anonymized usage events (e.g., practice started, check-in completed)
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data

Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. We do not send any journal content, mood scores, or personal text to Firebase. For details, see firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

AI provider (Yandex Cloud)
When you request an AI-powered practice recommendation, a short, anonymized description of your current mood state is sent to our AI provider. No names, journal text, or identifying information are included in these requests. Responses are generated in real time and not stored on external servers.

RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage subscription purchases. RevenueCat may receive your App Store or Google Play purchase receipt and a random app user ID. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any content from the App. For details, see revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple App Store / Google Play
Subscription billing is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not store or process your payment information.

4. Push notifications

If you enable reminders, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) or Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) to deliver local and remote notifications. A device push token is generated and used solely for delivering your requested reminders. We do not use push tokens for advertising.

5. No advertising or tracking

We do not run ads in the App. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID). We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers. We do not engage in cross-app tracking.

6. Data retention and deletion

All on-device data (journals, check-ins, preferences) is retained until you uninstall the App. Firebase Analytics data is retained for 14 months per Google's default settings. Crash logs are retained for 90 days.

Because the App does not create user accounts and does not store personal data on our own servers, there is no account deletion flow — your data lives on your device and is fully under your control.

7. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

8. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the UK, or California, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Because most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. For any data held by third-party services listed above, you may contact those services directly or contact us at sergeynoma@gmail.com and we will assist you.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Data sent to third-party services is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). On-device data is protected by your device's built-in security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
